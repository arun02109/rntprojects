Our Services


RNT PROJECTS provides Cathodic Protection Solutions of diversified nature across lengths and breadths of the country encompassing miles of cross-country pipeline, tank bottoms, buried vessels, plants, jetties and other off shore structures. 

Our Philosophy


Exceeding customer expectations in everything we do and holding ourselves accountable at every step. Embracing change and innovation and looking for new ways to grow our organization and ourselves.

 

Our Mission


 Our Mission is to achieve focus on our energy and resources on creating the preeminent solution for unique corrosion problems. To Dedicate ourselves in controlling corrosion by providing on – time, zero defect, safe, and environment Friendly Cathodic protection system to reduce environment hazards, which cause impact globally. 

Our Team

 We are backed by an efficient team, which is committed to cater to the diverse requirements and demands of the clients. Their meticulous and sincere efforts have enabled us in attaining the maximum satisfaction of the clients. RNT PROJECTS personnel are highly experienced in the field of cathodic protection, cross country pipelines On shore and Off shore, plant piping, steel tankage bottom and underground bullets etc. Well versed in various field surveys like Soil resistivity, Current requirement / drainage survey, coating conductance survey, Pearson survey, Close interval potential logging survey, Interference survey, DCVG Survey and Topographical surveys etc., the company has provided accurate results to support the CP works / installations.
